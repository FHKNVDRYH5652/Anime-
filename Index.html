How It Works
Step-by-step guide to using UNIX Predict
1

Open the Dashboard

When you open UNIX Predict, the tool automatically loads the last 100 WinGo 1-Min results from the live server. These results are updated every 5 seconds in real-time.

2

Watch the Results Bar

The scrollable results bar shows all 100 past results. Old results are on the LEFT, newest results appear on the RIGHT. When a new result comes in, it slides in from the right automatically.

3

Click PREDICT NEXT

Press the ⚡ PREDICT NEXT button. The AI will take 5-10 seconds to run 4 analysis engines: Pattern Scanning, Frequency Check, Logic Processing, and Infinity Calculation. Wait for all 4 to complete.

4

Read the Prediction

The result panel shows your predicted NUMBER (e.g., 4), SIZE (Big/Small), and COLOR (Red/Green/Violet). The number shown is the most likely outcome. Below it you see the size and color tags. Confidence % shows how strong the signal is.

5

Place Your Bet Smartly

Use the prediction to make your bet on the WinGo 1-Min game. Always start with the minimum bet amount and increase only if you are confident. Never bet more than you can afford to lose.

6

Track Your Results

Go to the History page to see all your past predictions, whether they were WIN or LOSS, and how accurate the tool has been over time. Use the Statistics page to understand patterns better.

7

Download Analysis Data

Click the small 📥 DATA button (bottom right corner) to download the complete pattern analysis chart in JSON format. Useful for advanced users who want to study patterns further.
